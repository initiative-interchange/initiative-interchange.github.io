Merry Christmas

Let's share the gift of education with everyone.

This Christmas, we want to make giving a bit easier and more meaningful. By selling Christmas cards designed by our artistic team, we raise funds to support those who are less lucky and do not have access to education.

Preorder now: donations@initiative-interchange.org

We are cooperating with UNICEF (unicef.at/interchange) to reconstruct schools in Aleppo, Syria – an area in which education is needed most to avoid a vicious cycle caused by an uneducated generation after the civil war. With only 5€ (approx. 3 Christmas cards), you can give one child a future!

Preorder now: donations@initiative-interchange.org

Just drop as an e-mail with your order (design and amount) and your contact details (name, address, phone number). We will be in touch with you shortly to confirm your order. Also, if you have any questions, don't hesitate to reach out! Merry Christmas.
